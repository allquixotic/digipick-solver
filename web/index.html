Digipick Solver

Instructions

This is a solver for the Digipick mini-game in Bethesda's Starfield (2023). Code: Click Here

Digipick game has 32 possible positions. They are represented in this tool by numbers from 0 (top center) to 31 (the position immediately to the left of top center), with the number increasing clockwise.

Click "Add Pick" and fill in the pins of each pick you have based on their zero-based index. Put a comma between each number.

Click "Add Ring" and fill in the holes of each ring you have based on their zero-based index. Put a comma between each hole position.

Click "Solve" and the output will be displayed, or an error if no solution could be found.
